Core values

Each value pairs mission-focused action with measurable outcomes for students, staff, and the wider community.

Faith-centered formation

Daily worship, chaplaincy, and spiritual direction nurture compassionate leaders.

  • Morning prayer and Eucharist
  • Retreats that connect faith with research
  • Peer mentoring in gospel values

Integrity & service

We cultivate ethical decision-making and service learning across provinces.

  • Community engagement with parishes
  • Transparent governance and reporting
  • Volunteer pathways for every student

Excellence & innovation

Creative labs, research partnerships, and digital learning shape resilient graduates.

  • Innovation sprints and project showcases
  • Co-teaching with regional universities
  • Scholarships for breakthrough ideas

Community & care

Whole-person support ensures every learner belongs and thrives.

  • Counselling and resilience coaching
  • Family-inclusive education plans
  • Safe residences with pastoral support
