Vision 2030

Shaping Papua New Guinea's future through Christ and Education

Our strategic plan outlines bold goals for academic innovation, campus expansion and community impact over the next decade.

Download plan

Strategic pillars

Access & equity

Provide Scholarships, remote learning hubs and mobile training to reach every provinces of Papua New Guinea

Academic Innovation

Introduce bachelor programs in education, health promotion and sustainable enterprise by 2028.

Research & Innovation

Establish a Highlands Research Centre focused on climate resilience, peace building and indigenous knowledge.

Campus Development

Construct a green technology park, multipurpose sports complex and expanded residential halls.

Digital Transformation

Deploy blended learning platforms, e-portfolios and analytics to personalise learning.

Global Partnerships

Strengthen collaborations with universities in Australia, the Philippines and the Pacific.

Roadmap

2026 – 2030

Accreditation renewal, campus master plan, and launch of online short courses.

2026 – 2027

Construction of solar micro-grid, new library wing and teacher research incubator.

2028 – 2030

More Bachelor Degrees, global immersion programs and Port Moresby Innovation Fund.

Key commitments

Student experience

Every student receives a personalised learning plan, leadership coaching and career support.

Community impact

Annual service hours will double through partnerships with parishes, health centres and youth groups.
