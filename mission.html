Mission Statement

Educating servant leaders for Papua New Guinea

Papua New Guinea Christain Institude of Higher Education forms women and men who embody Gospel values, academic excellence and cultural intelligence in every village, parish and workplace they serve.

Explore Vision 2030

Our mission

We commit to provide transformative Catholic tertiary education that:

  • Integrates faith, reason and Melanesian wisdom
  • Equips graduates to address literacy, health, entrepreneurship and governance challenges
  • Prioritises community engagement, ethical leadership and lifelong learning

Core values

Faith

We celebrate the sacraments, prayer and scriptural reflection as the heart of our learning community.

Excellence

We pursue outstanding teaching, research and service, constantly evaluating our impact on Port Moresby and PNG.

Respect

We honour every culture, language and story, promoting dialogue and peace-building.

Innovation

We embrace technology, entrepreneurship and creative problem-solving rooted in local realities.

Service

We dedicate time, talent and resources to the most vulnerable communities.

Stewardship

We care for the environment through sustainable practices and community education.

Formation pillars

Academic excellence

Rigorous programs aligned with DHERST ensure graduates are job-ready and future-focused.

Pastoral care

Small discipleship groups, counselling and retreats accompany every student.

Community engagement

Service-learning projects address literacy, health and economic empowerment.

Leadership & ethics

Workshops and mentorship cultivate integrity, accountability and peace building.

Prayer for PNGC

Lord Jesus, bless PNGC Institute of Higher Education. Fill our hearts with wisdom, compassion and courage so we may serve National Capital District and Papua New Guinea with humility and hope. Amen.
