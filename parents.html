Frequently asked questions

What items should my student bring?

Provide bedding, personal toiletries, formal wear for liturgies, laptop/tablet and any prescribed medication. Dormitories supply desks, lockers and study lamps.

How do I send care packages?

Use the campus postal address or send with PMV drivers travelling to Port Moresby. Please label packages clearly with the student’s full name and dormitory.

Can families visit?

Yes! Weekend visiting hours are 10am – 4pm. Please check in at the security gate and bring ID. Overnight stays require approval.

How do I track progress?

Parents receive quarterly progress reports via email. You can also request meetings with lecturers or the dean of students.
