About Dr. Pio

As Director of PNG CIHE, Dr. Pio guides institutional vision and academic quality, ensuring our programs serve both Papua New Guinea and the international community. Her work blends data-driven improvement with a heart for ministry.

She collaborates closely with departments to cultivate student leaders, strengthen governance, and expand access to tertiary education rooted in Christian values.
