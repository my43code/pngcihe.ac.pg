Who we are

About Papua New Guinea Institute of Higher Education

Papua New Guinea Institute of Higher Education exists to develop leaders who respond courageously to the social, economic and spiritual needs of Papua New Guinea. Our integrated curriculum balances academic rigor, pastoral care and Melanesian cultural intelligence.

Explore our mission

Institutional identity

We are chartered by the Christian Principles and aligned with DHERST. Our culture is rooted in stewardship, hospitality and excellence.

Holistic formation

We nurture intellectual curiosity, spiritual growth and professional competence through pastoral accompaniment and peer mentoring groups.

  • Weekly house meetings and chaplaincy support
  • Ignatian-style reflection and journaling
  • Community-based research in Port Moresby parishes

Leadership

We teach and mentor future leaders to serve with integrity and vision.

  • Joint teacher training with Papua New Guinea Christian Institute of Higher Education

Milestones

1981 – Founding

PNG CIHE opened as a teacher training centre to meet our country's urgent need for qualified educators.

2024 – University affiliation

Signed partnership with the University of Phillipines to align curriculum, supervision and credit transfer pathways.

2025 – Campus expansion

Construction of the Learning Facalities.

Values in action

Faith & Integrity

Daily prayer, Eucharist and counselling ensure students discern their vocation and lead with honesty.

Excellence & Innovation

Project-based learning, innovation sprints and peer observation drive continuous improvement.

Community & Care

We prioritise trauma-informed support, community development projects and family-inclusive education plans.

Meet our community

Students represent 22 provinces, bringing languages, customs and talents that enrich our learning community. Teachers and chaplains accompany students individually through growth plans.
