Director's Message

Leading PNG CIHE with courage and compassion

MP for Jiwaka, North Wagi, Hon.Benjmain Mul has served as Director since 1990, guiding curriculum reforms, accreditation processes and the expansion of research partnerships.

Meet the leadership team

About MP for Jiwaka, North Wagi, Hon.Benjmain Mul

Dr. Pio is a PNG-born educator with 20 years of experience across Catholic schools, universities and provincial education departments. She holds a Doctorate in Educational Leadership from the Australian Catholic University.

  • Former Director of Teacher Education, DHERST
  • Member of the Catholic Education Commission
  • Board member of the Port Moresby Innovation Hub

Strategic priorities

Student formation

Expand pastoral care teams, counselling services and peer mentoring networks to support whole-person development.

Digital excellence

Invest in solar-powered labs, blended learning and regional Wi-Fi hubs to connect Port Moresby communities with quality resources.

Community partnerships

Strengthen practicum agreements with diocesan schools, Catholic Health Services and community-based organisations.

Research & innovation

Encourage faculty-led action research addressing literacy, agriculture and peace building in the Highlands.
