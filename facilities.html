Campus infrastructure

Spaces that inspire learning and service

Our Port Moresby campus blends traditional architecture with modern, technology-enabled classrooms, ensuring students have the resources they need to learn, live and grow.

Schedule a tour

Campus highlights

Learning Commons

Flexible study areas, podcast booths and the eGranary digital library provide 24/7 access to thousands of journals.

Innovation labs

Equipped with high-speed satellite internet, 3D printers and robotics kits for STEM projects and community problem solving.

Residential villages

Safe, supervised dormitories with counselling rooms, study hubs and chapels on every floor.

Teaching practice centre

Model classrooms where students design lessons, test maker-space activities and host children from local schools.

Gallery

Campus services

Health & wellness

On-site clinic staffed by Catholic Health Services nurses, mental health counsellors and nutrition workshops.

Sports & recreation

Multi-purpose courts, fitness trail and inter-house competitions keep the community active.

Chaplaincy

Daily Mass, adoration, retreats and sacramental preparation led by our resident chaplain and campus ministry team.

Food services

Fresh produce from the campus garden and Port Moresby farmers supply our dining hall, bakery and coffee shop.
